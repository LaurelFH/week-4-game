Crystal Gem Count: The Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are 4 gems below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You will win the game by matching your totial score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each gem.
